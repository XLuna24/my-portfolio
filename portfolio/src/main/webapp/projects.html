Project Azul

Virtual Reality is fascinating as you can be inside of a game instead of just seeing through a screen. The usual vr headset and controller helps you dive into these vast worlds. The problem is many people get sick playing in these games as when they run in these games their body feels as if its moving although its not. With treadmills it help and helps with the immersion. treadmills cost a lot for the average consumer and cheap alternative wich is cybershoes does not give you full leg movement. Thats where Project Azul comes in. Its a type of treadmill shoe that lets you walk in any direction and give the user full body tracking in game.
